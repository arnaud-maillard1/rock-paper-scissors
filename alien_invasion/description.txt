Alie Invation

In Alien Invation, the player control a ship that appears at the bottom center of the screen.
the player can move the ship right and left using the arrow keys dan shoot bullets using keys 
spacebar. when the game begins, a fleet of aliens fills the sky and moves across and down
the screen. if the player shoots all the aliens, a new fleet appears that moves faster than the
previous flee. if any alien hits the player's ship or reaches the bottom of the screen, the player
loses a ship. if the player loses three ships, the game ends.
